TelcoObservabilityAPI is implemented by WSO2 Integrator: MI and registered in
WSO2 API Manager Service Catalog. The supplied publish-observability-api.sh also
creates/updates and publishes the managed APIM facade with:

  Name: TelcoObservabilityAPI
  Version: 1.0.0
  Context: /observability/v1
  Endpoint: http://wso2-mi:8290/observability/v1

The internal telco-observability:8088 service is never published directly.
